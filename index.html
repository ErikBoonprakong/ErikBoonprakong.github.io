Welcome to Big One Bakery! We are a small team of high level bakers, based in south-east London, who specialise in making giant and tasty pastries, cakes, pies, and almost anything else you can think of. If it can be baked, it can be baked big!

Big One Bakery is the perfect place to go if your looking for something to really spice up a special occasion. Or perhaps you just have an insatiable sweet tooth, either way, we are so confident in our standards that we guarantee your satisfaction, or your money back!
